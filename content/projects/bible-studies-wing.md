## Overview

Bible Studies Wing is a mobile application designed for bible study groups. It allows users to create accounts, read and share bible verses, and connect with other users.

## Features

- **User Accounts**: Create and manage user profiles.
- **Bible Reading**: Access and read bible verses.
- **Social Sharing**: Share verses with friends.
- **Community**: Connect with other users for discussions.

## Technologies Used

- **Mobile App**: Developed as a mobile application.
